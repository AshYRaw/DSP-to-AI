The SSM Evolution
From S4 to Hybrid Dominance

Four years of breakthroughs: how structured state spaces went from academic curiosity to production-grade architectures

Era 1 — The Foundation (2020–2021)
HiPPO: High-order Polynomial Projection
NeurIPS 2020
Albert Gu et al. discover that optimal online function approximation via Legendre polynomials yields a specific matrix A (the HiPPO matrix) whose eigenvalues provide provably optimal signal history compression.
💡 DSP insight: This is optimal pole placement for temporal memory — the Butterworth equivalent for signal history, not frequency response.
S4: Structured State Spaces for Sequences
ICLR 2022
The breakthrough paper. S4 combines HiPPO initialization with a key algorithmic insight: LTI state spaces can be computed as convolutions during training (parallel via FFT) and recurrences during inference (sequential, O(1)/step). First model to match Transformers on Long Range Arena with O(n) complexity.
Sequential MNIST: 98.3%
Path-X (16K): 88% (Transformers fail)
💡 DSP insight: S4 exploits the IIR↔FIR duality — train with truncated impulse response (FIR/convolution), infer with recursive state (IIR/recurrence).
Era 2 — Simplification & Understanding (2022)
S4D / DSS: Diagonal State Spaces
NeurIPS 2022
Discovered that diagonalizing A to diag(λ₁,...,λ_N) preserves nearly all performance while massively simplifying computation. Each state dimension becomes an independent first-order IIR section. This makes the DSP connection explicit: partial fraction expansion = parallel first-order sections.
💡 DSP insight: Diagonalization IS the partial fraction decomposition of H(z). Textbook DSP, discovered independently by ML researchers.
H3: Hungry Hungry Hippos
ICLR 2023
Stacks two SSM layers with a multiplicative gate between them. Achieves language modeling quality closer to Transformers. Identifies the critical gap: LTI systems can't do content-based selection — they treat every input identically.
💡 The "selective copy" failure: ask an LTI SSM to copy a specific token → it fails completely because it applies the same transform regardless of content.
Era 3 — The Mamba Breakthrough (2023–2024)
🐍 Mamba (S6): Selective State Spaces
Dec 2023 / COLM 2024
The landmark paper by Gu & Dao. Makes B, C, Δ input-dependent, breaking the LTI assumption but enabling content-based selection. Hardware-aware parallel scan replaces FFT convolution. First SSM to match Transformers at language modeling.
Throughput: 5× Transformer
Training: 40× faster than naive
Memory: O(1) per step
💡 DSP insight: Mamba converts the LTI IIR filter into a Linear Time-Varying (LTV) adaptive filter — like LMS/RLS, but driven by input content and trained via backprop.
Mamba-2 (SSD): State Space Duality
May 2024
Proves SSMs and masked linear attention are mathematically equivalent through semiseparable matrices. Chunked algorithm: quadratic attention within chunks, SSM state between chunks. 2–8× faster training than Mamba-1.
State size: N=128 (vs N=16)
Training: 2–8× faster
Leverages: Tensor cores
💡 The unification: SSMs and Attention aren't different paradigms — they're different views of the same matrix class (semiseparable matrices).
Era 4 — The Hybrid Revolution (2024)
Jamba: Hybrid Transformer-Mamba
ICLR 2025
AI21 Labs establishes the 1:7 attention-to-Mamba ratio at scale (52B params). Attention for retrieval, Mamba for efficiency. "Never put attention at the front." KV-cache reduced from 32 GB to 4 GB at 256K context.
💡 The recipe: Mamba first (efficient feature extraction), sparse attention later (content-based retrieval). IIR for bulk processing, FIR for precision.
ConMamba / ConExtBiMamba
ICASSP 2025
Replace MHSA in Conformer with bidirectional Mamba for ASR. WER competitive with Conformer at 40–45% less training time and 50% less memory. Critical finding: standalone Mamba underperforms — needs the convolutional module.
LibriSpeech clean: 3.9% WER
Training time: −45%
Long-context WER: −50%
Griffin, Bamba, Samba-ASR
2024–2025
Griffin (DeepMind): gated recurrences + local attention, matches Llama-2 at 6× fewer tokens. Bamba (IBM, 9B): Mamba-2 layers + 3 attention layers. Samba-ASR: pure Mamba encoder-decoder, 1.17% WER on LibriSpeech clean.
Era 5 — Edge Deployment & Future (2025+)
XAMBA / eMamba: SSMs on Edge NPUs
Feb 2025
First frameworks optimizing Mamba for commodity NPUs and FPGAs. Replace SiLU with piecewise-linear, cumsum with MatMul. 260 tok/s on Intel NPU, 11.6× perf/area on ASIC. Fixed-state, no-softmax, static shapes — architecturally ideal for NPUs.
Production Hybrids at Scale
2025
Jamba 1.5 (398B total, 94B active), Falcon Mamba 7B (5.5T training tokens), Codestral Mamba (256K context for code), Microsoft Phi-4-mini-flash-reasoning (Mamba + sliding-window attention + GatedMU, 10× throughput for reasoning).
💡 Hybrid architectures are becoming the default foundation model design. The question isn't "attention vs SSM" — it's "what ratio and where."
